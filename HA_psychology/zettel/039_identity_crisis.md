# Identity Crisis

Who am I becoming?

Normative crises:
- Adolescence: Initial identity
- Midlife: Reassessment
- Late life: Integration

Triggered by:
- Role changes
- Loss
- Failure
- Success (!)

Healthy crisis:
Exploration → Commitment

Unhealthy:
Stuck in confusion.
Or rigid foreclosure.

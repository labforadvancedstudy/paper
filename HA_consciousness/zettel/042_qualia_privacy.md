# The Privacy of Qualia

Your red might be my blue.
We'd never know.

We both call it "red."
We both stop at red lights.
We both pick red apples.

But the inner experience?
Completely private.
Unshareable.

This privacy is not a bug.
It's the essence of consciousness.

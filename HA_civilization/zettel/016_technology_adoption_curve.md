# Technology Adoption Curve

Adoption follows S-curve:
- Slow start (innovators)
- Rapid growth (early majority)
- Saturation (laggards)

Each technology compresses the timeline:
Writing: 5000 years
Printing: 500 years
Internet: 50 years
Smartphones: 5 years

Civilization accelerating its own acceleration.

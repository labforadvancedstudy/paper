# Stress Response

Fight, flight, or freeze.

HPA axis:
- Hypothalamus: Danger detected!
- Pituitary: Release ACTH!
- Adrenal: Cortisol flood!

Short-term adaptive:
- Energy mobilization
- Enhanced focus
- Immune boost

Chronic maladaptive:
- Immune suppression
- Memory problems
- Mood disorders

Modern problem: Tiger stress for email threats.
